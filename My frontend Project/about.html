About Ambydates Syrup

Welcome to Ambydates Syrup, your go-to destination for Family size, pocket size and big size jar. Our mission is simple: to bring the rich, golden sweetness of nature to your table in its purest form.

Ambydates was born out of a love for high-quality, organic products that nourish both the body and the soul. With a focus on sustainable sourcing, every bottle of syrup is crafted with care, preserving the essence of the finest dates. Whether you're drizzling it over pancakes, adding it to smoothies, or baking with it, Ambydates Syrup is here to elevate your culinary creations.

Our Journey

Founded in 2022, Ambydates started as a family project to share the taste of the Middle East's finest dates with the world. Over time, our passion evolved into a mission to create a product that is not only delicious but also ethical. From the farms where our dates are grown to the hands that bottle our syrup, we prioritize quality and sustainability at every step.

Why Choose Ambydates?

  • 100% natural and organic ingredients.
  • Sustainably sourced dates from trusted farms.
  • Crafted with care and a commitment to excellence.
  • Versatile for cooking, baking, and beverages.

At Ambydates, we believe that every drop of syrup tells a story of tradition, craftsmanship, and flavor. We invite you to join us on this journey of taste and wellness.
